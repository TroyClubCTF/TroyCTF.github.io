Introduction

In the past decade, computer security has become fundamental to our daily lives. Many programs have surfaced to address the need for cyber defense awareness, but few have really focused on the opposite: how to attack.

We created ClubCTF because we believe that learning how to attack is a necessary supplement to understand how to defend.

A CTF (short for Capture The Flag) is a special kind of information security competition that combines knowledge from programming, web development, cryptography, stenography, operating systems, and other principles of computer science in order to hack vulnerable programs. These competitions are typically played in teams of up to five people. When a challenge is successfully exploited, it will display a flag (piece/string of text) to redeem for points for your team.

We hope that you will find learning different and interesting ways to break programs as captivating as we have.
